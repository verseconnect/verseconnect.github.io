About Me


I’m a Toronto-based graphic designer with over 10 years of professional experience in visual communication. I began my career working with some of the top creative agencies in Trinidad, my home country, where I developed a strong foundation in branding, layout, and campaign-driven design. Over the years, I’ve delivered projects across the Caribbean region, ranging from billboard design to full campaign rollouts. My work has been successful across both social media and print platforms, including features in my country’s national newspaper and a print advertisement in The Wall Street Journal. Building on this experience, I went on to start my own agency called VerseConnect that specializesin event flyer design, art directing Caribbean events in New York, Trinidad, Barbados, and Toronto. Now settled in Toronto, I continue to grow and expand my practice, bringing cultural insight, strategic thinking, and high-impact visuals to every project.
